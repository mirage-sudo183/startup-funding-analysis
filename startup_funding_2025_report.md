# 2025 Startup Funding Analysis Report

## Executive Summary

2025 has been a landmark year for startup fundraising, with **AI dominating nearly 50% of all global venture funding** (up from 34% in 2024). Total AI sector investment reached **$202.3 billion**, a 75%+ increase year-over-year. Meanwhile, other sectors showed mixed performance, with cleantech declining significantly while defense tech hit all-time highs.
